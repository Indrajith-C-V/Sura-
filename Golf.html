GOLF GTI





The Volkswagen Golf GTI's key specifications typically include a 2.0L turbocharged petrol engine producing around 261 horsepower and 370 Nm of torque, a 7-speed automatic transmission, and a front-wheel-drive system. It has a length of approximately 4,289 mm, a wheelbase of about 2,627 mm, and a boot space of around 380 litres. Key features often include a McPherson strut front suspension, a four-link rear suspension, and 18-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels.
